Island of Pathiramanal

Kerala is truly the land of twisted trails to the world who have just discovered it. We are talking about the Island of Paathiramanal, which is located in the Vembanad Lake. It is one of the many fascinating islands in Kerala. We cannot stop talking about the number of green sceneries you will come across and you will have everything you ever wanted to see: rare birds, swaying coconut palms, whispering waters and whatnot. You will love to come back here once you go visit this place. It is actually a great break from the tiring work you do! So get to see the thick forests, paddy fields, green farmlands on this island. Ideal for: Stressful Professionals who like to take a break from their usual work schedule. Location: 31 km drive from Kottayam.

Nadukani

If you are a patron of ethereally beautiful places, then you need to go visit Nadukani. Another popular hill station near Kottayam, this is a major picnic spot. It offers wonderful views and major landscapes are covered with dense forests. So, you get to see a lot of greenery. Apart from that, Nadukani emerged as one of those new offbeat places which every college-going kid wanted to go and all the couple who wanted to seek some refuge. Ideal for: Family, Youth, and Couples Location: You can take taxis or buses to go to Nadukani from Kottayam. If you have a self-drive vehicle, then that would be great too!
